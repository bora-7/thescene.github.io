Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have several rights with regard to your personal data:

  • a. Right to access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • b. Right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccuracies in the personal data we hold about you.
  • c. Right to erasure: You have the right to request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal conditions.
  • d. Right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, subject to certain legal conditions.
  • e. Right to data portability: You have the right to receive the personal data you have provided to us in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, or to request that we transfer it to another data controller.
  • f. Right to object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or when our processing is based on legitimate interests.
